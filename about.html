About

Our Story

EarthenKnot was born from a passion for handcrafted creations and the belief that every handmade piece tells a unique story. We started with a simple goal—to bring beautifully crafted, sustainable, and meaningful products to people who appreciate authentic craftsmanship.

Every product is handcrafted with love—either by me or by talented women who once started creating simply as a hobby. Today, that passion is turning into something much bigger: an opportunity to earn, grow, and build confidence through their creativity.

This is more than just a product launch. It's a step towards supporting handmade artists and celebrating craftsmanship.

EarthenKnot Lovingly woven for you.

An Invitation to Artists

Collaborate

And now, I'd love to invite you to be part of this journey.

If you create handmade products—whether it's journals, diaries, greeting cards, bookmarks, crochet items, paintings, home décor, or anything made with your own hands—I would love to collaborate with you.

Your products will be showcased and sold under your own brand name through our platform, giving your work the audience it deserves while you continue doing what you love.

Let's grow together, support local artisans, and build a community where creativity is valued and rewarded.

If you're interested, feel free to reach out. I'd be honored to work with you.
